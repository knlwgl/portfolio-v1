**Technologies:** Fairlearn, Tensorflow, NumPy, pandas

- Developed a Python script to automate membership inference attacks on identically set up unconstrained and constrained models, allowing for efficient investigation of privacy leakage caused by enforcing fairness.
- Demonstrated that enforcing performance-based fairness metrics has a greater effect on model privacy leakage.
- Explored how models with low complexity, such as Decision Trees, experience greater privacy leakage when enforced with performance-based fairness interventions, stemming from a higher susceptibility to over-fitting.
- Visualised the results of the experiments using Pyplot in order to clearly map the privacy implications.
