title: "Smoker-Drinker Model Analysis"
publishedAt: "2024-02"
summary: "Built neural networks to predict smoking and drinking status from Korean health data."
---

**Technologies:** Python, Tensorflow, scikit-learn, pandas, Git

- Processed 991,346 samples of biological health data collected by the National Health Service of Korea.
- Trained Feed-Forward Neural Networks to predict an individual's status as a smoker or drinker, or both.
- Collaborated with a team to analyze several classifier models and identify reasons for disparities.
