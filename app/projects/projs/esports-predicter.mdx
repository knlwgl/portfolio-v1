**Technologies:** Python, scikit-learn, pandas

- Processed data from more than 125,000 League of Legends Esports games using the pandas library for training.
- Produced a model that predicts the outcome of future matches using the Ridge Classifier model and Sequential Feature Selector from scikit-learn with 77% accuracy, 0.773 F1 Score and 0.231 MSE.
- Utilized classifier to finish in the top 10% of all predictors of the 2024 League of Legends World Championship.
